My brand in one word: Overachiever.

Seriously, the last time I received a grade less than an A was the third grade! I also consistently receive feedback from my managers that I have by far exceeded their expectations, both in quality of work and timeliness.

I'm currently a full time student at Utah Valley University, where I have discovered an intense passion for cybersecurity. Besides studying for classes and/or certifications, I'm also a very active member of the Utah Valley University Cybersecurity Club and recently joined the leadership team!

Joining the Cybersecurity Club has helped me to participate in various capture the flag competitions, including National Cyber League, as well as monthly "Saturday Sparring" red team events hosted by the club and Utah Open Source.
